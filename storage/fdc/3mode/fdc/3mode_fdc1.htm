3MODE FDC1

[This is preliminary documentation and subject to change.]

SUMMARY

This FDC sample is a floppy disk controller for the Toshiba 3Mode series. The sample works with the following models:

Toshiba EQUIUM 5160D1
Toshiba EQUIUM 5200D1
Toshiba EQUIUM 5200M1
Toshiba EQUIUM 6200D1
Toshiba EQUIUM 6260M1
Toshiba EQUIUM 6230D1
Toshiba EQUIUM 5230D1
Toshiba EQUIUM 5200D2
Toshiba EQUIUM 5230M1
Toshiba PV3000/5000/5500 series

Other Toshiba models are covered by the 3mode\Fdc.2 sample. The sample is located in the directory \\Ntddk\Src\Storage\3mode\Fdc. FDC resides at the bottom of the driver stack and performs the low-level interactions with the physical device. Typically, FDC routines are called by the driver that occupies the next level up in the driver stack, possibly the Flpydisk sample.

Note: The controller can be used with a class driver other than Flpydisk, such as a floppy tape driver.

Fdc does not compile in 64-bit. It is compatible with x86 32-bit only.

BUILDING THE SAMPLE

Type build in the \\Ntddk\Src\Storage\3mode\Fdc directory. A successful build produces Tosdfdc1.sys.
